Pith Voice is a voice journal that runs entirely on the user's iPhone.

WHAT THE APP DOES
1. Records audio (AVAudioEngine).
2. Transcribes speech on-device using Apple's Speech framework (iOS 26+).
3. Generates a short summary and 2-4 thematic tags on-device using Apple's FoundationModels framework (Apple Intelligence, iOS 26+).
4. Persists entries locally via SwiftData with NSFileProtectionComplete.
5. Offers a paid subscription or lifetime IAP (hard paywall, no free trial).

HOW TO VIEW / TEST THE IN-APP PURCHASES (fastest path)
Open the Settings tab (bottom-right tab bar) and tap "View subscription options". The paywall appears immediately and shows all three In-App Purchases:
- Pith Annual - com.hadger.pith.sub.annual - $59.99/year (auto-renewable)
- Pith Lifetime - com.hadger.pith.iap.lifetime - $99.99 one-time (non-consumable)
- Pith Weekly - com.hadger.pith.sub.weekly - $4.99/week (auto-renewable)
Each plan has a purchase button. The Subscription Disclosure block, Restore Purchases button, and Privacy Policy / Terms of Use links are all visible on the same screen.

ALTERNATIVE PATH (main flow)
The paywall also triggers in normal use: on the Today tab, tap the Record button, then Stop (this creates an entry; speaking is optional). Do this twice. On the third Record tap the paywall is presented. Two free entries precede the hard paywall.

VERIFYING THE ON-DEVICE CLAIM
The app works fully in airplane mode after first launch. Reviewers can verify zero outbound traffic via any network proxy. The only outbound traffic is StoreKit (Apple-controlled).

DEVICE REQUIREMENT
Pith Voice targets iPhone 15 Pro or newer. Recording and entry creation do not require Apple Intelligence to be enabled; on-device summarisation degrades gracefully if it is unavailable, and entries are still created, so the paywall remains reachable.

PERMISSIONS
- Microphone: for recording entries.
- Speech Recognition: for on-device transcription.
- Notifications (optional): weekly digest only, opt-in.
